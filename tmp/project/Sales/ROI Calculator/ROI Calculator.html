Methodology

Every assumption, sourced

The default percentages draw on published industry research. Tune them to the client's own data — these are starting points, not promises.

    This calculator produces directional estimates for discussion, not a guarantee of results or a contractual commitment. Hard-dollar drivers are modeled as a percentage of contingent labor spend; soft-dollar drivers as recovered administrative hours valued at the blended loaded rate you set. Industry estimates for unknown spend use illustrative contingent-share and rate benchmarks and should be replaced with client actuals. The 1.5% platform rate is a standard planning figure. Figures display in the selected country's currency; cross-currency benchmark conversions use illustrative exchange rates for planning only.
